The 2016 Michigan Baja Team, led by Cal Salisbury, looks to continue the winning tradition of the Michigan Baja Racing team. Team members represent all class levels on campus and a wide variety of majors - anyone is welcome to join! Weekly meetings are held on Mondays during the school year at 7pm in the Wilson Student Team Project Center conference room. Contact Zech Schneider (zechsch@umich.edu) for more details.
